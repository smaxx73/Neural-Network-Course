_ARTI_ Course Structure (12 sessions × 2h = 24h)
====
2026 Version

This course is designed to teach engineering students the fundamentals of machine learning, focusing on the perceptron and multi-layer perceptrons (MLPs). The structure emphasizes intuitive understanding before introducing mathematical rigor, ensuring students grasp core concepts effectively.

### **Module 1: Foundations (4 hours)**

**Session 1 (2h): Python Basics for ML** ✅ Done
- Variables, functions, NumPy fundamentals
- Vectors and matrix operations

**Session 2 (2h): Perceptron & Linear Classification** ✅ Done  
- Activation functions, manual calculations
- Geometric interpretation, decision boundaries

---

### **Module 2: Learning Without Calculus (4 hours)**

**Session 3 (2h): Perceptron Learning Rule** ← **NEXT**
- Why automatic learning?
- Rosenblatt's update rule (intuitive, no calculus)
- Implementation and visualization
- Convergence behavior

**Session 4 (2h): Multi-Layer Networks (Intuitive)** ← **NEW APPROACH**
- Solving XOR with hidden layers
- Forward propagation (just computing outputs)
- Manual weight adjustment experiments
- Why we need systematic training → motivation for gradients!

---

### **Module 3: The Mathematics of Learning (6 hours)**

**Session 5 (2h): Loss Functions & Gradient Descent**
- NOW we introduce the math (students understand WHY)
- Loss as "how wrong we are"
- Gradients and optimization
- Linear regression example

**Session 6 (2h): Backpropagation**
- Chain rule
- Automatic gradient computation
- Training MLPs properly

**Session 7 (2h): Logistic Regression & Softmax**
- Binary and multi-class classification
- Cross-entropy loss
- Complete classification pipeline

---

### **Module 4: Practical Deep Learning (6 hours)**

**Session 8 (2h): Generalization & Regularization**
- Train/val/test splits
- Overfitting detection
- Regularization techniques

**Session 9 (2h): Optimization Techniques**
- SGD, momentum, Adam
- Learning rate schedules
- Batch normalization

**Session 10 (2h): PyTorch Introduction**
- Tensors and autograd
- nn.Module
- Rebuilding previous models

---

### **Module 5: Real-World Applications (4 hours)**

**Session 11 (2h): Advanced Topics**
- CNNs (image classification) OR
- Other architectures depending on interest

**Session 12 (2h): Final Project & Best Practices**
- End-to-end pipeline
- Professional practices
- Project presentations

---
